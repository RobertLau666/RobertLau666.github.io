Chenyu Liu 刘晨雨

I am a master degree candidate in Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My supervisor is Researcher Ran He. Prior to attending the Institute, I obtained a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University.


My research interests lie in machine learning, deep learning. The primary goal of my research is to design effective learning algorithms for deep forgery detection in audio-visual fusion. In addition, I am interested in applications of reinforcement learning, such as computer games and robotics.
